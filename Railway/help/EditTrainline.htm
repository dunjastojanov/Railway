Edit a trainline

To edit a trainline you must folow the steps bellow:

  1. If you wish to add more stations from a dropdown menu pick the station and after conformation the station will appear on the middle part of the screen.
  2. Repeat the procces and add the next station the same way.
  3. Between the stations added there will be two filelds to input the duration of the trip between the stations, and the price of the ticket between the stations. Fill those fields with the requierd information.
  4. Repeat this process of adding the next station and filling in the above metioned fields until you have created the trailine you desire.
  5. If you wish to change price or duration between already added stations simply click on the field between those stations and type in the new correst number.
  6. Click on the save button and the window will appear that will display information about the trainline you have edited.
  7. If you see the correct information click on the yes button. After conformation station will be edited and you will be taken to a page that displays all of the trainlines.

Price

Price represents a price of the trip from one station to the other. If the passenger is going through mulitiple stations until he gets to his destination, the prices are simply added up.

Duration

Duration represents a number of minutes that it takes the train to get from the one station to the other. It is used to caculate the time train will be at each station.
