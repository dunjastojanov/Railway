List of trains

On this page you are able to see all of the trains available in the system. For each train name, number of wagons, number of rows of seats per wagon, and number of columns of seats per wagon are displayed.

Adding a new train

To add a new train click on the add new train button.

Editing a train

To edit a train click on the button that says edit and it is located left of the name of the train name you would like to edit.

Deleting a train

To delete a train click on the button that says delete and it is located left of the name of the train you would like to delete.

Undo and redo

You can undo the last operation you have done by clicking on the undo button.

You redo the last operation you have undone by clicking on the redo button.
