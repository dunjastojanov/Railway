Choose seat

Choosing seats for ticket the user is buying

To choose a seat you must follow the steps bellow:

  1. From a dropdown menu pick the number of wagon you want to travel.
  2. From a panel choose seat by clicking on it. Lightblue seats are available, white seats are already taken and orange seats are seats you have chosen
  3. You can disregard selected seat by clicking on it again. This action turns seat lightblue showing it is available again.
  4. On the top left you can see how many seats are left to choose depending on how many seats you have chosen to buy.
  5. On the bottom left you can see number of seats you have chosen.
  6. By clicking on 'Save' button you can confirm and save seats you have picked.
