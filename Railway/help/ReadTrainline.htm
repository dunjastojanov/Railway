List of trainlines

On this page you are able to see all of the trainlines available in the system. For each trainline name and all of the stations that it goes through are displayed, as well as the duration and price of the trip between two consecutive stations.

Adding a new trainline

To add a new trainline click on the add new trainline button.

Editing a trainline

To edit a trainline click on the button that says edit and it is located left of the name of the trainline you would like to edit.

Deleting a trainline

To delete a trainline click on the button that says delete and it is located left of the name of the trainline you would like to delete.

Price

Price represents a price of the trip from one station to the other. If the passenger is going through mulitiple stations until he gets to his destination, the prices are simply added up.

Duration

Duration represents a number of minutes that it takes the train to get from the one station to the other. It is used to caculate the time train will be at each station.

Undo and redo

You can undo the last operation you have done by clicking on the undo button.

You redo the last operation you have undone by clicking on the redo button.
