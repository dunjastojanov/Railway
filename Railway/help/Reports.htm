Sold tickets for the month

If you wish to review report for tickets sold for the month click on the radion button that says "Sold tickets for the mont"

Next choose a month and a year from dropdown menu and click on the show button.

In the table below you will see information about the tickets sold on the month and the year you have picket.

Sold tickets for a ride

If you wish to review report for tickets sold tickets for a ride click on the radion button that says "Sold tickets for a ride"

Next choose a trainline that you want to see information for.

Then choose a start and end date in which time period the system will search and click on the show button

In the table below you will see the information for the tickets sold for chosen trainline in chosen timeperiod.
