Search ticket

To find and buy a tucket you must follow the steps bellow:

  1. From a dropdown menu choose a stations you are traveling from.
  2. From a dropdown menu choose a stations you are traveling to.
  3. Pick a date at which your trip will be.
  4. With the plus and minus button controll the number of seats you want your ticket to apply for.
  5. Click on the search button and the availble tickets will appear.
  6. Chose the ticket you would like to buy and click on the buy button. After conformation you will have succesfully bought a ticket.
