Search ticket

To find and buy a ticket you must follow the steps bellow:

  1. From a dropdown menu choose a stations you are travelling from.
  2. From a dropdown menu choose a stations you are travelling to.
  3. Pick a date on which your trip will be.
  4. With the plus and minus button control the number of seats you want your ticket to apply for.
  5. Click on the search button and the available tickets will appear.
  6. Choose the ticket you would like to buy and click on the buy button. After conformation you will have succesfully bought a ticket.
