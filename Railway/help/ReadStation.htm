List of stations

On this page you are able to see all of the stations available in the system. For each station name, longitude and latitude are displayed.

Adding a new station

To add a new station click on the add new station button.

Editing a station

To edit a station click on the button that says edit and it is located left of the name of the station you would like to edit.

Deleting a station

To delete a station click on the button that says delete and it is located left of the name of the station you would like to delete.

Undo and redo

You can undo the last operation you have done by clicking on the undo button.

You redo the last operation you have undone by clicking on the redo button.
